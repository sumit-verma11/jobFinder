## Notes for the LLM generating cover notes

- Keep it to 4 lines max.
- Don't invent a recruiter's name — open with "Hi," or similar, not "Hi [Name],".
- Lead with relevant stack overlap for the specific role.
- Use only real, concrete facts from profile.md — never invent numbers, technologies, or experience.
- Describe any demo/portfolio projects accurately (seeded data vs. real users/transactions).
- Never mention CTC or notice period in a cover note.
- End with clear, low-pressure interest.
